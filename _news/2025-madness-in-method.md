Registration is now open for the [Madness in Method](https://events.humanitix.com/madness-in-method) workshop, the main event of the [*homo calculans* project](https://homo-calculans.blog). The workshop takes place at the University of Melbourne on September 10, and features keynotes from [Prof Kath Bode](https://researchportalplus.anu.edu.au/en/persons/katherine-bode) and [Dr Luca Sholz](https://lucascholz.com/).
